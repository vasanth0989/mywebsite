Privacy Policy


Your privacy is important to us, and we go to great lengths to protect it.

SBK is committed to fair information practices. SBK respects the privacy and security of our users. Our goal is to deliver the information, resources, and services that are most relevant and helpful to you with regard to our tradeshows.

In order to achieve this goal, we sometimes collect information during your visits to understand what differentiates you from each of our other users. We will not disclose it to any person or organization outside SBK, except as described in this Privacy Policy.

To demonstrate our commitment to your privacy, we have prepared this statement disclosing our privacy practices. Here, you will learn what identifying information of yours is collected, how and when we might use your information, how we protect your information, who has access to your information, and how you can correct any inaccuracies.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

SBK collects certain information from and about its users three ways: from our Web server logs, with cookies, and directly from the user.

  • 1. Web server logs: When you visit our SBK website, we may track information to administer the site and analyze its usage. Examples of information we may track include:

    • a. Internet protocol addresses that visit the site

    • b. What kind of browser or computer has visited the site

    • c. The number of links clicked within the site

    • d. The state or country from which users access the site

    • e. The date and time of visits

    • f. The name of user’s Internet service provider

    • g. The Web page from which user links to the site

    • h. The pages viewed on the site

  • 2. Cookies: Our websites use cookies (i.e., small bits of text that our servers place into a file on your computer's hard drive). This is used to save your preferences and login information, and provide personalized functionality. You can reject cookies by changing your browser settings, but be aware that this will disable some of the functionality on our websites.

  • 3. Business information provided by the user: On some of our websites, visitors can register to attend our tradeshows as either an exhibitor or buyer, register for a seminar, and participate in surveys or contests. When you register, we will ask you for your business information (information already in the public domain).

If you're registering as an exhibitor or for a seminar, we may request financial information (e.g., What is your credit card number?). Any financial information we collect is used only to bill you for the products and services you purchased. If you purchase by credit card, this information may be forwarded to your credit card processor and provider and is not stored by SBK.

For all types of registrations (exhibitor, buyer, seminar), we will ask for your business information including; name, company name, address, email address, fax number and telephone number. We may then contact you with information about other SBK products and services.

If you register as an exhibitor, your information will be shared with relevant affiliated third party trade vendors approved by SBK to facilitate your participation in our tradeshows and printed in the official show directory both online and available at the show

If you register as a buyer, your information will be shared with relevant affiliated third party trade vendors approved by SBK to service the show.

If you register for a seminar, your business information may be shared with the seminar presenter.

Finally, you may be asked to disclose information to us so that we can provide assistance and information to you. For example, we may collect information from you (such as an email address, system information, and problem descriptions) in order to provide online technical support and troubleshooting.

If you don't want to receive promotional materials from SBK, you can opt out at any time. You simply need to update your preferences with each SBK service to which you are registered. Please direct all opting out questions and requests to info@sbklondon.com

If you don’t want to receive promotional materials from any of SBK’s affiliated third party trade vendors, please contact them directly.

SBK also may provide summary statistics about our customers' interests and preferences, products sold, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third parties. These statistics do not include personal identification information; they are used only to enhance the design of our website and customize new product offerings to our customers

How We Protect Your Information

SBK uses technical security measures to prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of information under our control

For example, when we ask customers to provide financial information (such as a credit card number) that data is protected using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SBK does not store your credit card information.

Who Has Access to the Information?

SBK will share your information in three ways:

  • 1. As required by law, in a matter of public safety or policy, or as needed in connection with the transfer of other business assets

  • 2. With other SBK tradeshows

  • 3. With affiliated third party vendors who use it on SBK's behalf or in connection with their relationship with us

Links to Other Companies' Sites

Our websites contain links to sites maintained by companies that are not affiliated with SBK. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any linked sites, and we encourage you to use caution when visiting them. We recommend you view the privacy policy posted on each site.
