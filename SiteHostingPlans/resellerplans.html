Reseller Hosting

Reseller hosting is a type of web hosting in which the account owner has the potential to use his or her allotted hard drive space and bandwidth to host websites. That means that one will act the role of being a middle person. Most of the web hosts that are not using reseller accounts themselves offer reseller web hosting. This plan provides you to resell cheap packages to your own clients. It means that you will gain advantage of the other resources available in your site to earn money by reselling them to your clients. The reseller buys the host's services wholesale and then sells them to customers, for a profit. Basically, web hosting reseller can be web developer or systems integrator who provides web hosting as an additional service. Reseller hosting does not seek better knowledge of the technical aspects of web hosting. A reseller is responsible for solving with his or her own customer platform, but any hardware and connectivity issues are basically forwarded to the server provider from whom the reseller plan was bought. The company may also get into extra services like search engine optimization, web development, web design, etc. Meanwhile the fees with major hosts are only a few dollars per month, and it is a low-margin business. But, as web hosting is one of the biggest online businesses, every website needs hosting.
